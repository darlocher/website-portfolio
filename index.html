About

From interviewing journalists assaulted by police to writing a submission to the UN Working Group on Arbitrary Detention, I combine digital fluency, creative storytelling, and rigorous research to create impactful campaigns and drive change.

My work spans multiple domains including human rights advocacy, election research, data visualization, and political analysis. I have extensive experience working with organizations such as the Committee to Protect Journalists, PEN America, Rock the Vote, and the Associated Press.

I am passionate about using data-driven approaches to promote democracy, free expression, and human rights globally. My background includes both quantitative research methods and hands-on advocacy work across multiple countries and cultures.
